# Privacy Policy

**Anime Watchlist Tracker**
*Last updated: March 1, 2026*

## Overview

Anime Watchlist Tracker ("the App") is a personal anime tracking application for Android. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App handles and how it is used.

## Data Collection

**The App does not collect, store, or share any personal data.** There are no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

## Local Storage

All watchlist data — including anime titles, watch status, episode progress, and ratings — is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to uninstall the App or clear its data.

## Network Requests

The App makes network requests to the [Jikan API](https://jikan.moe/) (an unofficial MyAnimeList API) solely to:

- Search for anime by title
- Retrieve anime details (synopsis, episode count, cover art, genres, score)
- Check for new episodes and sequels (when notifications are enabled)

These requests contain only the search query or anime identifier. No personal data, device identifiers, or usage analytics are transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the Jikan API as its only external service. Jikan's own privacy practices are governed by their terms. No other third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect user data are used.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the changes will be reflected in this document with an updated "Last updated" date.
