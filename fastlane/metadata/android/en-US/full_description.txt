Keep track of every anime you're watching, planning to watch, or have completed — all from a single, lightweight app.

Anime Watchlist Tracker is a personal anime management app built for fans who want a fast, private, and straightforward way to organize their anime library. No account required, no ads, no data collection.

FEATURES

• Organize anime by status: Watching, Completed, Plan to Watch, On Hold, or Dropped
• Track your episode progress with quick +/− controls
• Rate anime on a 1–10 star scale with tap or swipe
• Search the MyAnimeList catalog to discover new shows and add them to your list
• View anime details including synopsis, genres, cover art, and MAL score
• Sort your list by title, score, progress, rating, or date added — ascending or descending
• Filter search results by watchlist status
• Enable per-anime notifications to get alerts when new episodes or sequels are detected
• Offline-first — your entire watchlist is stored locally on your device
• Material You theming — adapts to your wallpaper on Android 12+

PRIVACY

Your data stays on your device. The app makes network requests only to search for anime and check for updates — no personal information is ever collected or shared. See our full privacy policy for details.

Anime data is provided by the Jikan API, an unofficial open-source API for MyAnimeList.
