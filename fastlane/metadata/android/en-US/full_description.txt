Anime Watchlist Tracker helps you manage your anime watching journey from start to finish.

Features:
• Browse and search anime from MyAnimeList via Jikan API
• Add anime to your personal watchlist with custom watch status
• Track watch order and season details for long-running series
• View streaming links for each title in one place
• See broadcast times in your local timezone
• Expandable streaming link lists for titles with many sources

Stay organised and never lose track of what to watch next.
